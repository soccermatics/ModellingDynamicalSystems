Challenge I:1

Calculate the mean value for yearly income

The sum of incomes (in thousands of pounds) is

math

12+22+23+31+34+36+40+52+62+106 = 418

This means that the sum of all the incomes is $418,000$. Dividing by the number of people (which is 10) gives an average income, $418,000/10=41,800$ pounds.
